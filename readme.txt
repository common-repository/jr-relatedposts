== Description ==

This related posts plugin allows you to show your related blog posts underneath your content - To keep visitors on your site!

In the administration panel, you have the option to choose the number of related posts you wish to show, as well as the title above the related posts.

This related posts plugin works very simply by retrieving other posts in the same category as the post the user is currently viewing, which should be able to keep your user browsing more posts on your site and therefore keeping them for longer!

== Installation ==

1. Upload `/jr-relatedposts.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Edit the JR RelatedPosts Settings in your Administration Panel
4. Done!
